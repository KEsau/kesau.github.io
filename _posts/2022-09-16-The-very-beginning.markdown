This is my first blog article, in memory of those idyllic golden days in [Kyoto][kyoto], Japan.

Most posts in the site will be written in English though it's not my mother tongue. 
In some special cases, for example, sharing Chinese poetry, the entire context will be in Chinese (maybe with poor translation).

The site is using the default theme of [Jekyll][jekyll]. 
I'm trying to make it rich in content rather than in format.

这是我的第一篇博客，我用它纪念在[京都][kyoto]的日子，那时我无忧无虑。

我以汉语为母语，但本站的主要内容将用英文写作。
在某些特殊情形下，例如，分享中文诗歌，文本将完全是是中文（可能有翻译，但质量差）。

本站使用[Jekyll][jekyll]的默认模板。我努力使它的内容超过形式。
